Train 3D Model

Kidwind Worlds

Info website!

During this project we were tasked to create a wind turbine that would be efficient, powerful, and resistant to stress. We then competed against other teams from all over the world to see who had the best design. My team and I won the Rookie of the Year Award for our design which very pleasing to me and my team. Our winning design was a 3 bladed wind turbine with a 12.5 degree angle of attack, using mostly 3D printed and machined parts.
